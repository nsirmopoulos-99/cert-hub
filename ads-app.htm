The App Campaign Ecosystem

Google App Campaigns (formerly UAC) use machine learning to make decisions for you. You don't target keywords or placements manually. You provide Goal + Assets + Budget, and Google does the rest across its largest properties.

INVENTORY

Where Ads Run

🔎📺🛍️
  • Google Search: Matches intent.
  • Google Play: Search & "You might like".
  • YouTube: Native video & shorts.
  • Display Network: 3M+ sites/apps.
  • Discover: Feed environments.
AUTOMATION

The Machine Learning Core

You cannot set manual bids for specific keywords or placements. Google analyzes 300M+ signal combinations in real-time.

Inputs You Control:
  • Creative Assets (Text, Video, Image)
  • Bidding (Target CPA/ROAS)
  • Location & Language
⚠️

Exam Concept: The Learning Period

App Campaigns need time to learn. Avoid making significant changes (budget/bid > 20%) during the learning phase. It typically takes conversion delay + 2 weeks to stabilize.

Campaign Types & Bidding

There are three main types of App Campaigns. Choosing the right one depends on your business maturity and goals.

Pro Strategy: Ad Groups

You can have multiple Ad Groups in an App Campaign. Use them to theme your assets.
Example: Ad Group A = "Food Delivery", Ad Group B = "Grocery Delivery". This keeps ads relevant to specific user intents.

Measurement & Tech

App measurement is more complex than web. You need an SDK (Software Development Kit) to talk to Google Ads.

How to Track Conversions

  • 1. Google Analytics for Firebase (Recommended): Seamless integration, free, unlocks audience lists automatically.
  • 2. App Attribution Partners (AAP): Adjust, AppsFlyer, Kochava, Branch. Required if you use many ad networks.
  • 3. Google Play (Android only): Track installs directly (no code required), but can't track iOS.

Deep Linking

A deep link sends a user who already has your app to a specific page inside the app, rather than the app store or home screen.

Exam Tip

For App Engagement (ACe) campaigns, Deep Links are mandatory. You cannot run re-engagement ads without directing users to specific content.

iOS & SKAdNetwork

On iOS, privacy rules (ATT) limit data. Google integrates with Apple's SKAdNetwork to measure aggregated results. You must configure your conversion value schema to track quality on iOS.

Optimization Strategy

Once the campaign is live, how do you manage it? Remember: The machine needs data stability.

Rule #1: The Budget Change Limit

Do not change your daily budget by more than 20% at a time. Larger changes can reset the machine learning phase.

Rule #2: Bid vs. Budget

Your daily budget should be at least 50x your target CPA (for Installs) or 10x your target CPA (for In-App Actions).
Example: If tCPA is $2, Daily Budget should be $100+.

Rule #3: Creative Refresh

Check asset performance reports ("Best", "Good", "Low"). Replace "Low" performing assets every 2-3 months. Do not delete them all at once; cycle them gradually.
