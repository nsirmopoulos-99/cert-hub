2. Create Ad Set Structure

Configure your three core audiences: Cold, Warm, and Scaling.

Ad Set 1: Interest Targeting

Targeting new people based on platform behavior.

Yoga AND Meditation Broad Theme

Tip: Avoid mixing vastly different interests.

Exclude these to force the system to find NEW people:

Website Visitors (180d) Purchasers (180d)
Select Appropriate Placements (e.g., FB/IG Feed, Stories, Reels)

Ad Set 2: Remarketing

Retargeting people who engaged but haven't bought.

Website Visitors (30d)
Instagram Engagers (90d)

Don't show "Buy Now" ads to recent buyers:

Purchasers (180d)
Select Appropriate Placements (e.g., FB/IG Feed, Stories, Reels)

Ad Set 3: Lookalike (Scaling)

Targeting new people who look like your buyers.

Lookalike (US, 1%) - Purchasers

Keep it purely prospecting:

Website Visitors Purchasers
Select Appropriate Placements (e.g., FB/IG Feed, Stories, Reels)

Why Exclusions Matter?

● Overlap: Without exclusions, you pay twice to reach the same person in "Interests" and "Remarketing".

● Strategy: Exclusions force the algorithm to find net new people in Cold/LAL ad sets.
