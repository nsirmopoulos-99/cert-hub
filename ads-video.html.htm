Video Format Lab

Selecting the right ad format is the #1 skill tested. Formats align with specific marketing goals. Use the filters to learn which format fits where.

Bumper Ads

6 Seconds

Non-skippable, 6 seconds max. Designed for high reach and frequency.

Bid Strategy: Target CPM.

Non-Skippable In-Stream

15 Seconds

Users cannot skip. Guarantees your full message is seen.

Bid Strategy: Target CPM.

Masthead

Homepage

Massive reach on the YouTube Homepage. Bought on a reservation basis (CPD/CPM), not auction.

Outstream

Mobile Only

Ads that play on partner sites/apps (GVP), usually without sound until tapped.

Bid Strategy: vCPM.

Skippable In-Stream

Versatile

Plays before/during video. User can skip after 5s. If goal is Consideration, you pay per View (CPV).

Trap: A "View" is 30s or the full ad (if shorter).

In-Feed Video

Discovery

Thumbnail appears in search results or "Up Next". Users click to watch.

Bid Strategy: CPV.

YouTube Shorts

Vertical

Full-screen vertical video (under 60s). Massive organic engagement. Ideal for mobile action.

Included in: Video Action Campaigns, Video Reach.

Video Action Campaigns

Conversion

Multi-format container: Skippable In-Stream + In-Feed + Shorts. Adds CTAs and End Screens.

Bid Strategy: Target CPA.

Bidding Strategy Matrix

Video bidding is unique. Understand when to pay for a "View" vs. an "Impression" vs. an "Action".

Target CPM

Cost Per Mille. Use for Awareness (Bumpers, Non-Skip) or Video Reach Campaigns (which mix formats).

Maximum CPV

Cost Per View. You pay when a user watches 30s (or full ad) OR interacts. Use for Consideration.

Target CPA

Cost Per Action. You pay for conversions. Use for Video Action Campaigns (VAC).
