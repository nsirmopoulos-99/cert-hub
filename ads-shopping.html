Foundations: GMC & Ads

Understanding the ecosystem. Merchant Center stores the data; Google Ads builds the showroom. This section covers the fundamental differences between Search and Shopping and the setup process.

Search Ads 🔍

  • • Text-based ads.
  • Trigger: Keywords you select.
  • • Intent: Research & Specific Queries.

Shopping Ads 🛍️

  • • Visual ads (Photo, Price, Store).
  • Trigger: Product Data (Feed) matching.
  • • Intent: High purchase intent.

The Google Merchant Center (GMC) Flow

⚠️ Exam Tip: You target Shopping ads using Product Data, NOT keywords. This is the most common trick question!

Performance Max & Bidding

Modern Google Shopping relies on Performance Max (PMax) and Smart Bidding. Understand when to use which strategy.

Performance Max (Retail)

A goal-based campaign accessing ALL inventory (Search, Shopping, YouTube, Display, Maps, Gmail).

⚠️ Exam Tip: You don't "target" audiences in PMax; you provide Signals to guide the AI.

Bidding Strategy Matrix

Map your goal to the strategy.

Target ROAS (tROAS)

"I want $5 revenue for every $1 spent."

Requires Conversion Value tracking.

Maximize Conversion Value

"Get the most revenue possible within my budget."

Best for growth if no strict ROAS target.

Policy & Exam Tips

Avoid disapprovals and account suspensions. Memorize these common traps.

The "Big 3" Disapprovals

💸

Price Mismatch

Feed price ($10) ≠ Landing Page price ($12). Immediate disapproval.

Fix: Enable Automatic Item Updates.
📦

Availability Mismatch

Feed says "In Stock", Page says "Sold Out".

🚫

Promotional Overlays

Images must be clean. No "Free Shipping" text or logos overlaid on the photo.

Exam Traps ⚠️

  • • Targeting: Shopping uses Feed Data, not keywords.
  • • Negative Keywords: Can be used in Standard Shopping, hard to use in PMax.
  • • Local Ads: LIA requires a separate inventory feed and Google My Business link.

Misrepresentation

The most severe violation. Causes suspension.

  • Missing contact info.
  • Insecure checkout (Http).
  • Hiding shipping costs until checkout.
