Core Campaign Elements

Liquidity is key. Restricting placements, audiences, or budgets reduces liquidity and hurts performance.

Campaign Structure

  • Campaign: Objective, Buying Type, CBO.
  • Ad Set: Audience, Placement, Budget (if manual), Schedule, Bidding.

Automation (Meta Advantage)

  • Complete Solutions: Advantage+ Shopping (ASC), Advantage+ App Campaigns.
  • Single-Step: Advantage+ Placements, Audience, Creative.
Unit 2

Buying Types

🔨 Auction

Maximizes efficiency & flexibility.

  • Pricing: Dynamic CPM (Fluctuates).
  • Bid Strategies: Highest Volume, Highest Value, Cost per Result, ROAS, Bid Cap.
  • Best for: Always-on, Direct Response.

📅 Reservation (Reach & Freq)

Maximizes predictability.

  • Pricing: Fixed CPM. Guaranteed Delivery.
  • Frequency: Target Frequency or Cap.
  • Sequencing: Tell a story in order (Ad 1 -> Ad 2).
  • Constraints: Min 200k reach. No exclusions allowed.

Budget Strategy

Advantage+ Campaign Budget (CBO)

Centralized budget. System distributes to best ad sets. Best for efficiency. Don't use if you need strict spend limits per audience.

Lifetime Budget

Required for Ad Scheduling (Dayparting). System spends more on high-opportunity days.

Unit 5

Audience Strategy

Advantage+ Audience (Broad)

Allows AI to find audience beyond your inputs. You provide suggestions (Age 18-35), but system can go broader (35+) if it finds cheaper conversions.

Custom Audiences (Warm)
Sources: Website (Pixel), App (SDK), Customer List (Hashed Email), Engagement (Video views).
Lookalike Audiences (LAL)

Reaches new people similar to your best customers. Source must have 100+ people.
1% = High Similarity (Narrow). 10% = High Reach (Broad).

Unit 6

Placements

Advantage+ Placements

Recommended. Delivers to all available placements (FB, IG, Messenger, AN). System finds cheapest result.

Manual Placements

Use only for strict Brand Safety (e.g. avoid Audience Network) or specific creative limitations.

Unit 7

Ad Creative

Carousel: Storytelling, multiple products.
Collection: Mobile-only, product grid.
Instant Experience: Fast-loading native page.
Creative Fatigue: Performance drops due to overexposure.
Fix: Refresh creative, expand audience, or use Advantage+ Creative.
Unit 8

Campaign Reporting & Measurement

1. Lift Tests (Incrementality)

Brand Lift: Measures Recall, Awareness via surveys (Test vs Control).
Conversion Lift: Measures Incremental Sales. Gold standard. Uses a holdout group that sees NO ads.
A/B Test: Compares variables (Creative A vs B). Deterministic.

2. Advanced Modeling

Marketing Mix Modeling (MMM):

Statistical analysis of sales vs ALL marketing (TV, Radio, Meta, etc.). Good for budget allocation across channels. Privacy-resilient.

Multi-Touch Attribution (MTA):

Assigns credit to multiple touchpoints in a journey. Good for understanding the path to purchase.

Unit 9

Data Privacy Policies

GDPR (EU) / LGPD (Brazil) Requires explicit consent for data processing. Can lead to signal loss.
CCPA (California) Right to opt-out of data sale. "Limited Data Use" flag restricts data usage.
HIPAA (USA) Prohibits sharing health data. Healthcare advertisers must not use Pixel for sensitive data.
Unit 10

Brand Safety Controls

  • Inventory Filters Expanded (High risk), Moderate (Default), Limited (Strict). Excludes sensitive content tiers.
  • Block Lists Prevent ads from running on specific apps/websites (Audience Network).
  • Topic Exclusions Prevent ads from appearing on Facebook In-Stream Video related to Gaming, Politics, etc.
Unit 11

Ad Review & Rejections

🚫 Prohibited (Rejected)

  • Personal Attributes: "You are 25" or "Meet other seniors". (Implies knowledge).
  • Sensational Content: Shocking, gore.
  • Misleading Buttons: Fake play buttons on images.
  • Unrealistic Results: "Lose 10lbs in 2 days".

⚠️ Restricted (Requires Permission)

  • Alcohol, Dating, Gambling.
  • Financial Services / Crypto.
  • Social Issues, Elections, Politics.
